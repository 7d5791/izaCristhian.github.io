About Me

I am a Full Professor and Coordinator of the Computer Science Undergraduate Program at Yachay Tech University (Ecuador). With a PhD in Telematics Engineering from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (Spain, 2018), I specialize in wireless networks, vehicular ad-hoc networks (VANETs/IoV), V2X communication, cybersecurity, and privacy-preserving protocols.

My research has been published in high-impact venues such as Sensors, IEEE Access, and ACM conferences.
